Not just another Jr developer

Hi I am Davis, a versatile engineer keen to begin my career as a front-end developer. I am passionate about building useful tools and solving challenging problems. I posses a wide range of skills from problem solving, critical thinking, excellent communication and team-working skills. With a growth mindset and enthusiasm for learning, enabling me to adapt quickly and be a valuable asset in any organization.
