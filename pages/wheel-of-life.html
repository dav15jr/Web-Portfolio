Wheel Of LifeWeb App

Self-Improvement App

This started out as a self-improvement tool I built while at Tryumph Coaching for my clients to help them evaluate their life and set goals to improve the parts they were not happy about.

It was initially built using excel before it was improved it and decided to developed a web version, with the help of a friend, to make it easier to access and use for users.

I plan to add authentication to the app so users can save their data, making it easier to track their progress.
